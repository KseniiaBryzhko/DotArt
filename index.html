Useful info

For a longer service life of products with dot and stained glass painting, you should follow some rules:

  • Covers and diaries should be wiped with a cloth moistened with water (not alcohol-containing liquids!).
  • Don't twist them too hard.
  • Painted glass products can be washed - carefully and only by hand with a soft sponge, in warm (not hot) water and without the use of abrasive products.
  • In no case should the products be soaked in water, put in the refrigerator and exposed to open flame.

Follow these recommendations, and painted products will serve you for a long time!
